Every massage I perform is unique, because every body is unique.

Tuina Massage

A traditional Chinese massage that uses rhythmic pressure techniques to balance energy and relieve tension.

Good for: Neck pain, fatigue, low energy, stress, back pain, poor mobility, headaches, menstrual pain, digestive symptoms, sports injuries

Cupping Therapy

An ancient Chinese practice that involves applying a plastic, glass, or bamboo cup to the skin to suck out the surface muscle layer and hold it within the cup.

Good for; Fatigue , Stress, Neck Pain, Headaches, Digestive Symptoms, Insomnia, Promotes General Health and Well-Being.
